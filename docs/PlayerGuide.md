# Player Guide

## Basics
*   `/xhelp` - View available commands.
*   `/spawn` - Teleport to the server spawn.
*   `/home` - Manage your personal home points.
*   `/warp` - Teleport to public warp points.
*   `/xgamemode` - Change your gamemode (if permitted).

## Teleportation (TPA)
*   `/tpa <player>` - Request to teleport to another player.
*   `/tpahere <player>` - Request another player to teleport to you.
*   `/tpaccept` - Accept a pending request.
*   `/tpadeny` - Deny a pending request.

## Friends
*   `/friend add <player>` - Send a friend request.
*   `/friend accept <player>` - Accept a friend request.
*   `/friend remove <player>` - Remove a friend.
*   `/friend list` - View your friends.
*   **Auto-Accept:** You can enable "Auto-Accept Friend TPA" in the Friend Settings UI.

## Teams
Create or join a team to play together!
*   `/team` - Open the Team Menu.
*   **Team Home:** Set a shared home for your team.
*   **Team Chat:** Use `/tc` or `/teamchat` to toggle private team chat.

## Economy
*   `/bal` or `/money` - Check your balance.
*   `/pay <player> <amount>` - Send money to another player.
*   `/shop` - Open the server shop.
*   `/ah` - Open the Auction House.
*   `/bounty` - Set a bounty on a player's head.

## Other
*   `/deathcoords` - View where you last died.
*   `/stats` - View your playtime, kills, and deaths.
